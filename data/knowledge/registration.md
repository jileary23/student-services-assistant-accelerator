# Registration and Academic Calendar

Fall registration opens April 15 for continuing students and May 1 for new students. Priority registration times appear in the student portal one week before registration opens. The standard add/drop deadline is 11:59 PM local time on the tenth calendar day of the term.

Students should review holds before registration. Advising, immunization, financial, or conduct holds can prevent enrollment. The Registrar's Office can explain a hold, but only the office that placed it can remove it.

Always confirm dates on the official academic calendar because accelerated and professional programs may use different schedules.
