# Financial Aid Documents

Students applying for need-based aid should submit the FAFSA and include the university's school code. The Financial Aid Office may request identity verification, tax documents, proof of citizenship or eligible noncitizen status, and household information.

Requested items and deadlines appear in the secure student portal. Students should upload documents only through the approved portal and should not send Social Security numbers or tax documents through chat or email.

Aid offers are made only by the Financial Aid Office. The assistant can explain published steps but cannot estimate, approve, deny, or change an award.
